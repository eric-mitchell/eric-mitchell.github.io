instrumental #1 [winter]

the mountains

Some pictures from Mount Rainier National Park (affectionately known as "Mommy Mountain") and Olympic National Park in early August. Starring my friend, Meir (and me). We rented the cheapest car we could find and crammed the following into 9 days (in order):

Rainier:

Emmons Vista Trail [via White River Campground]
Sunrise Hike [Visitor Center to Third Burroughs]
Paradise Hike [Visitor Center to Pebble Creek]

Olympic:

Mount Storm King [Lake Crescent]
Klahhana Ridge [via Hurricane Ridge Visitor Center]
Cape Flattery [Makah Reservation]
Shi Shi Beach [Makah Reservation]
Rialto Beach
Second Beach
Hoh Rain Forest [toward Five Mile Island]
Ruby Beach
Lake Quinault
Mount Ellinor/Lake Cushman [Olympic National Forest]

We also squeezed in a day and a half in Victoria, Canada (via ferry from Port Angeles) in the middle of the trip.
